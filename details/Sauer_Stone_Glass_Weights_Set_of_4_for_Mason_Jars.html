Fermentology Sauer Stones

Fermenting weights designed to keep your vegetables, fruits, or other ingredients fully submerged in the brine, preserving freshness and flavor. These glass weights are an essential addition for anyone interested in making homemade ferments with confidence.

Why Choose Sauer Stones?

Crafted from non-porous, food-safe glass, Sauer Stones resist contamination and ensure hygiene with every use. Their unique finger grip design makes them easy to insert and remove, streamlining your fermentation routine and reducing the effort needed to maintain clean, sealed containers.

Perfect Fit for Mason Jars

These weights are specifically designed for regular mouth mason jars, fitting snugly to create a perfect seal and protect your ingredients from air exposure. Whether you're making kimchi, sauerkraut, or other fermented foods, Sauer Stones are your reliable partner in achieving consistent, delicious results.

Key Benefits

  • Keeps vegetables submerged to reduce spoilage
  • Ensures a proper seal for your mason jars
  • Resists absorption of germs and maintains hygiene
  • Convenient finger grip for easy handling
  • Part of Fermentology's complete set of fermentation tools

Ideal for Home Fermentation Enthusiasts

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fermenter, Sauer Stones offer the perfect solution to enhance the quality and safety of your homemade ferments. They make the entire process more convenient, reducing the risk of mold and ensuring your food stays fresh for longer.
